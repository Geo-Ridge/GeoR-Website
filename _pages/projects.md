## Our Projects & Solutions

GeoRidge is actively developing and maintaining a range of geospatial solutions and open-source projects.

### Featured Projects


#### GeoR Traverse
A comprehensive surveying and traversal application for collecting and processing geospatial field data with high precision. Perfect for surveying projects, boundary mapping, and field verification.

**Repository**: [Geo-Ridge/geor_traverse](https://github.com/Geo-Ridge/geor_traverse)

**Features:**
- Real-time GPS/GNSS integration
- Point and line feature collection
- Traverse computation and analysis
- Export to standard GIS formats

---

#### GeoR Mission Planner
An advanced mission planning and drone management application. Design flight missions, optimize coverage patterns, and manage autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) operations.

**Repository**: [Geo-Ridge/geor_mission_planner](https://github.com/Geo-Ridge/geor_mission_planner)

**Features:**
- Interactive mission design
- Flight path optimization
- Coverage planning and analysis
- Real-time UAV monitoring
- Mission execution and telemetry

---

#### GeoR ODM Frontend
A modern web interface for Orthophoto and Digital Elevation Model (ODM) processing, visualization, and analysis. Built with cutting-edge web technologies for responsive performance.

**Repository**: [Geo-Ridge/geor_odm_frontend](https://github.com/Geo-Ridge/geor_odm_frontend)

**Features:**
- Interactive orthophoto viewer
- DEM visualization and analysis
- Data management and processing
- Export capabilities
- Web-based accessibility
